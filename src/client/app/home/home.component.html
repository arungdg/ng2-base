MESOFRE

A map based medical service application that can request, map and monitor the status. An integrated application focused towards the healthcare sector. It will be a single channel for all the medical services that may be needed in case of emergency or regular consultations. Such a connected system will help the service providers to connect with the patients and respond pro-actively based on the level of emergency.
